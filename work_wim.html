Brochure Design Inspired by Wim Crouwel

Phoroshop / Indesign / Illustrator

Wim Crouwel is a Dutch graphic designer and typographer. The design was inspired by his typography—extremely well planned and based on very strict grid systems. The goal was to design a promotional brochure and posters that are translating the mood and styles of Crouwel's design works visually through layout, image making, typography, and photo manipulation.
