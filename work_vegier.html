Vegier—Branding & Package

Vegier is a brand of Vegetable smoothies made fresh everyday using organic ingredients for customers on the go. The design inspiration came from natural way of how vegetable grows. The design on each bottle reflects the texture and color of each vegetable flavor which gives a playful, organic, and energetic look to the brand. The brand design communicates luxury and quality through look but keeps it approachable with the minimal and colourful package of the bottle.
