Synthesis Exhibition Branding

Synthesis is an exhibition revealing insights and exploring methods on each stage of the design process. Synthesis stands for the act of mixing, reflecting, and combining design directions and executed forms in order to make a final product. The exhibition is deconstructed and divided in the space to emphasize on the stages. The color and logo reflects the concept showing the process rather than final product. Brochure and promotional items also keep the look and feel of exhibition.
