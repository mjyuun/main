Synthesis Exhibition

Branding / Conceptualization / Photoshop / Illustrator / Indesign

SYNTHESIS is an exhibition revealing insights and exploring methods on each stage of the design process. Synthesis stands for the act of mixing, reflecting, and combining design directions and executed forms in order to make a final product. The exhibition is deconstructed and divided in the space to emphasize on the stages. By showing the process rather than final product, Synthesis models to show freedom to think beyond a hard cut formula. The process is seen as many avenues waiting to be explored and challenged, and each point is a possibility.
